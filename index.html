How to Sync Contacts

Recommended

Option 1: Live Sync (CardDAV)

Keep your contacts updated automatically! Whenever brand logos or shortcodes are updated on our server, your phone will sync them without redownloading.

For iPhone, iPad & Mac:

Get one-click setup using an Apple Configuration Profile:

  1. Tap Download Sync Profile.
  2. Open the Settings app on your device.
  3. Tap Profile Downloaded at the top.
  4. Tap Install in the top-right corner and enter your passcode.
Download Sync Profile

For Android & other devices:

Add a manual CardDAV account in your settings (or use a sync tool like DAVx⁵):

Server: sync.shortcodeicons.com
Username: public
Password: public
Port: 443 (SSL enabled)

Option 2: Offline Fallback

Download individual brand vCards manually. No accounts or sync settings required.

  1. Browse the directory of brands.
  2. Click the vCard button on any brand to download its individual .vcf file.
  3. Open the downloaded file to save it directly to your contacts list.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is 100% protected.

Short Code Icons runs entirely in your web browser. We do not host a database, we do not set tracking cookies, and we do not transmit any contact details or search queries to our servers.

When you click "Download," all vCard compilation, canvas image conversions, and file exports occur directly on your local device. We have no way of knowing which shortcodes you select or download.

Carbon Ads are served as static units and do not track your personal identity or access your local contact card data.
