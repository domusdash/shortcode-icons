How to Import Your Contacts

1

Download the vCard (.vcf) file

Click "Download All" or select specific cards and download. The `.vcf` file contains the contact details along with the brand logo image embedded directly inside.

2

Transfer/Open on your Mobile Device

iOS / iPhone: AirDrop the file from your Mac, or email it to yourself and tap the attachment. You can also send the file to yourself via iMessage.
Android: Email the file to yourself, save it to Google Drive, or transfer it to your phone's storage.

3

Import into Contacts app

iOS: When you open the file, tap "Add All Contacts" on the top of the contact list view. In a few seconds, the contacts will be imported with the company logos.
Android: Open the Contacts app, select "Manage Contacts" or "Settings," tap "Import/Export," choose "Import from .vcf file," and select the downloaded file.

4

Verify in Messages

Open your text messages list. The boring shortcodes and raw numbers will now show the brand names and colored logos, cleaning up your inbox!

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is 100% protected.

SMS Avatars runs entirely in your web browser. We do not host a database, we do not set tracking cookies, and we do not transmit any contact details or search queries to our servers.

When you click "Download," all vCard compilation, canvas image conversions, and file exports occur directly on your local device. We have no way of knowing which shortcodes you select or download.

Carbon Ads are served as static units and do not track your personal identity or access your local contact card data.
